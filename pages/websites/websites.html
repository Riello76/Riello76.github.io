Glastonbury Festival

This is the bootcamp project I attended, where I had the opportunity to showcase my web development skills. For this project, I recreated the Glastonbury Festival home page, adding a unique touch of personification to enhance user engagement. The site features a detailed PowerPoint presentation that outlines the project scope, objectives, and development process. Additionally, a comprehensive wireframe and mockup were created on Figma, providing a clear visual representation of the design and layout. This project demonstrates my ability to combine creative design with technical proficiency to deliver an engaging and functional web page.
