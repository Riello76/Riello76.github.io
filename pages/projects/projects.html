Streamer Website

Click to view

This site was designed for a streamer, making all social networks easily accessible. I practiced creating vector graphics and experimented with leather-like effects on elements. These techniques gave the impression of stitched visuals. While experimental, the project became an important step for refining my approach to digital design and interface creation.
