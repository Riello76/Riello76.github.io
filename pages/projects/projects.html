Projects

WADA Solutions

This site was an idea for a backend programmer, where I envisioned a suitable logo featuring two intersecting curly brackets. I also designed a business card and a calendar, which you can view in the branding section. This project allowed me to explore new design concepts and apply my skills in creating cohesive branding materials. Although it was an early attempt, I value it as a significant step in my journey towards mastering design and branding.

Streamer Website

This is a site that at the time I thought was for a streamer, where I imagined all social networks at my fingertips. Here I put new knowledge into practice, including vector graphics with Inkscape. I tried to give an effect on a surface of leather, where the elements seemed to be sewn onto the fabric. It was clearly an experiment, and maybe I could fix it better later.

Heat Wave

This site was my first real project, but I didn't have much experience and there are many mistakes. I had to imagine a company that dealt with plumbing and heating, create a logo (which I made with Paint 3D), choose the colour palette, and add the various sections as required by the project. I did my best at the time, but I didn't even know what a wireframe was. This site represents an important phase in my learning journey. It's a personal album of memories and growth, showcasing my initial steps in web design. The approach was driven by a desire to innovate and test new ideas, even if it meant making some mistakes along the way. I cherish it as a reminder of how far I've come and the foundation upon which I've built my current skills.

Glastonbury Festival

This is the bootcamp project I attended, where I had the opportunity to showcase my web development skills. For this project, I recreated the Glastonbury Festival home page, adding a unique touch of personification to enhance user engagement. The site features a detailed PowerPoint presentation outlining the project scope, objectives, and development process. Additionally, a comprehensive wireframe and mockup were created on Figma, providing a clear visual representation of the design and layout. This project demonstrates my ability to combine creative design with technical proficiency to deliver an engaging and functional web page.
