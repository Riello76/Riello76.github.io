Blender Workspace Creations

I'm currently exploring Blender through a project focused on recreating my real-life workstation. In this scene, I’ve applied screenshots of my actual monitors as textures on the 3D models, capturing the authentic look and feel of my setup. To enrich the environment, I'm also using BlenderKit, which offers a wide range of assets and tools that enhance the realism and depth of the virtual workspace.
