Blender Workspace Creations

I'm currently exploring Blender through a project focused on recreating my real-life workstation. In this scene, I’ve applied screenshots of my actual monitors as textures on the 3D models, capturing the authentic look and feel of my setup. To enrich the environment, I'm also using BlenderKit, which offers a wide range of assets and tools that enhance the realism and depth of the virtual workspace.

About This Page

This section is dedicated to my early explorations in 3D design, primarily using Blender. At this stage, it's more of a creative hobby than a structured design path, giving me the freedom to experiment without constraints.

I enjoy building simple environments and trying out new tools and techniques as I learn. While I haven’t developed full 3D projects yet, I see this as a space with potential — one I plan to explore more deeply in the future.
