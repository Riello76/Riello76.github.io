3D Design

Blender Workspace Creations

I'm still exploring Blender and currently working on a project to recreate my real workstation. In this project, I've used screenshots of my actual monitors as textures, applying them to the monitor faces in the 3D model. This approach helps me capture the true look and feel of my workspace in the virtual environment. I'm also leveraging BlenderKit to create and enhance these environments, which provides a range of assets and tools to further enrich my 3D workspace.
